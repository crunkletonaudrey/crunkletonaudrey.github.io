Funny Business

Funny Business is a short documentary I made as the director of photography. This film follows Jordanne Brown a local Toronto stand-up comedian in her journey as a female in the comedy industry, and trying to survive in the craziness that is Toronto.

Process

This documentary was made in a very short period of time since it was for a class, which involved a lot of multitasking and exceptional planning skills. Check out some of the hard work that went into this project below:
