Of Age - Video

  • Role: Co-Editor & 2nd AD / Script Supervisor

I worked on the short film Of Age, a coming of age film that follows a group of friends who reunite on a road trip. I worked on set as a script supervisor and 2nd AD. I also worked in post-production as a co-editor, in which I was responsible for editing half of the film.
